Welcome to the World of the Three-Section Staff

The three-section staff, also known as the Sanjiegun or Sansetsukon, is one of the most advanced traditional Chinese martial arts weapons. It is made of three wooden or metal rods connected by chains or rope. Three‑sectional staff (Chinese: sanjiegun) is a traditional Chinese martial arts weapon made of three wooden or metal rods connected by chains or rings. It is known for its high versatility, allowing both long‑range strikes and close‑range trapping techniques.

This website explains its history, origin, uses, advantages and training methods.
